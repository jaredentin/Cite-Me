Instructions

Groups

You must be in a group to interact with your friends.

There are 3 ways to join a group.


     1) Create your own
     2) Request to join one
     3) Have someone else request you

Any group request or invite must be confirmed. You are not restricted to one group.


Quotes

You can quote (or cite) any group member. This quote must be confirmed by that member.

A quote will appear between a group member's first and last name wherever shown in the browser.
